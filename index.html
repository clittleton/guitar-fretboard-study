Hints & Tips

Start With Landmarks

Memorize notes on frets 0, 3, 5, 7, and 12 first. They repeat and anchor your map.

Learn Open Strings Cold

Know E A D G B E instantly. Every fretboard answer grows from these six notes.

Use One String At A Time

Master one string up to the 12th fret, then move to the next string.

Say Notes Out Loud

Speaking the answer while clicking or typing reinforces memory faster.

Track Accuracy Before Speed

In free mode, focus on correct answers first. Add timers after your accuracy rises.

Sharps And Flats

Typed answers accept enharmonic spellings (for example, Db counts as C#).
